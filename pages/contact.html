Frequently Asked Questions

When is the admission deadline? +

The main admission deadline is typically in November of each year. However, applications are accepted throughout the year based on seat availability. Early applications are encouraged for better consideration.

What are the admission requirements? +

Requirements vary by grade level. Generally, students need to pass the entrance exam, submit required documents, and appear for an interview. For detailed information, please visit our Admissions page.

Do you offer scholarships? +

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships, need-based financial aid, and sports excellence grants. Contact our admissions office for more information about scholarship opportunities.

What is the school fee structure? +

Annual fees range from NPR 45,000 to NPR 55,000 depending on the program. Additional charges apply for meals, transportation, and activities. A detailed fee structure is available on the Admissions page.

Do you provide school transportation? +

Yes, we offer school transportation services covering multiple routes in and around Putalibazar. Transportation fee is calculated separately. Contact the office for route details and fees.

How can I schedule a school visit? +

You can schedule a campus visit by calling us at +977-74-550-123 or submitting the contact form on this page. We encourage prospective students and parents to visit our campus during school hours.
