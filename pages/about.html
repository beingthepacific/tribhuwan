Our History

Tribhuwan Aadarsha Model Secondary School was established in 1998 with a vision to provide quality education to the students of Syangja district. Over the past two decades, our institution has grown from a small school to one of the leading educational centers in the region.

Named after the great social reformer Tribhuwan, our school embodies the values of social responsibility, inclusive education, and community service. We have consistently maintained high academic standards while fostering the holistic development of our students.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation in teaching methodologies, infrastructure development, and community engagement. We take pride in the achievements of our alumni who have excelled in various fields and made significant contributions to society.

Key Milestones

  • 1998 - School established with 150 students
  • 2005 - Introduced +2 Science and Management programs
  • 2010 - Built new academic block with modern facilities
  • 2015 - Achieved ISO 9001:2015 certification
  • 2020 - Successfully transitioned to digital learning during pandemic
